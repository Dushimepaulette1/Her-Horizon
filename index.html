About HerHorizon

HerHorizon is dedicated to empowering young women in Rwanda by providing easy access to life-changing opportunities. We believe every woman deserves the chance to pursue her dreams and build a bright future.

Our platform connects you with scholarships, internships, mentorship programs, and skills training opportunities from trusted organizations and institutions.
